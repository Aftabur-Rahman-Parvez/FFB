Media Training

FFB has developed networking and partnership with existing journalist networks and organizations in Bangladesh for knowledge sharing and capacity building of national and regional level journalists on genetic engineering, transgenic crop and other applications of modern agribiotechnology.

Media Fellowship

FFB has implemented a competitive media fellowship program. Media fellows are assigned to publish evidence-based news articles about agribiotech issues. A long-term relationship has been established with the awarded journalists to effectively disseminate information on genetically engineered crops through media reports and coverage.
